About SSCMA

Our Mission

To provide Sprott students with opportunities to explore, learn, and excel in supply chain management through networking, professional development, and hands-on experience.

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization at Carleton University for supply chain management education and career development, preparing the next generation of industry leaders.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Industry partnerships with leading supply chain companies
  • Exclusive networking events and workshops
  • Case competition opportunities
  • Mentorship programs with industry professionals
  • Career development resources and job placement support
